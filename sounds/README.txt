Drop your background music file here, named exactly:

  music.mp3

That's it — music.js will pick it up automatically on page load and loop
it on by default. Any royalty-free or owned mp3 works. Suggested sources
for a cute/sweet vibe:

  - https://pixabay.com/music/search/cute/
  - https://pixabay.com/music/search/ukulele/
  - https://www.bensound.com/royalty-free-music/track/cute
  - https://www.chosic.com/free-music/romantic/

The other sound-effect files (click.mp3, wrong.mp3, correct.mp3,
collect.mp3, send.mp3) are optional and also go in this folder — the
site works fine without them, it just won't play those specific SFX.
